About GenieEats

Our Story

Founded in 2020, GenieEats started with a simple idea: make great food accessible to everyone. What began as a small operation serving a handful of neighborhoods has grown into a platform connecting thousands of restaurants with hungry customers across the city.


Today, we partner with over 500 restaurants, from beloved local favorites to popular chains, ensuring that whatever you're craving, we can deliver it fresh and fast.
